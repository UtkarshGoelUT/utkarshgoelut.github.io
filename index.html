    TiTa

    TiTa aims at bridging the gap between teachers and students by creating an online interactive environment. Teachers can create classrooms, share assignments, resources, schedule quizzes, and set timetables. On the other end, students can join classrooms, keep a check on their weekly schedule, view and submit solutions to assignments. They can also track their performance with analysis for each quiz they take. There is also an option to install it on your desktop and mobile in the form of PWA.
